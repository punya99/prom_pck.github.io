Thin Blade Rotary
The four bar rotary cutting creasing machine is suitable handling 3mm, 5mm as well as 7mm corrugation sheets. It is best suited for the industrial applications owing to its quality which are as per the international norms. The robustness and optimum performance provided by the machine makes it the focal point of the production capability.
Slotter
The slotter as the name suggests is suitable for slotting and corner cutting for corrugated boards. Slotting machine is used for cutting grooves, keys and slots of various shapes so as to allow the fitment of boxes especially mesh type in a way that is as per customer specifications.
Stitching Machine
Stitching machine or specifically referred to a s box stitching machine is equipped to handle client customization requests.
Wood Master
This machine is specialized for pallet making and designed for cutting plywood so that both Full Deck and Plank pallets can be made without much hassel.
Pneumatic Coil Nailer
Pneumatic Coil Nailer is specifically designed for pallet repair and manufacturing. Pneumatic nailers are also typically dedicated to specialty uses, such as framing and sheathing and finishing.
